BARANGAY 593

DESCRIPTION

A Barangay in the region of National Capital Region (NCR), within the city of Manila, under the administrative district of Sampaloc. According to the Census on the year 2020, the population of the Barangay is 1, 775. Estimated elevation above sea level is 8.6 meters (28.2 feet). The coordinates are 14.6012, 121.0108 (14° 36' North, 121° 1' East).
